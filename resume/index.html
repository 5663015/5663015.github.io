About Me

I am currently a member of DMT (Doctor Management Trainee) program in JD.com, Inc. I received my Ph.D. degree in Nation Space Science Center, CAS and University of Chinese Academy of Sciences. I received my B.S. in School of Intelligent Science and Engineering, Harbin Engineering University.

I was born in Handan city, Hebei Province, China in 1993. I love reading and I think reading can bring peace of mind. My goal in life is to understand the world as widely as possible and to contribute to it as much as possible. I'm an introvert and may be struggle to socialize. But introverts have a richer inner world and are better able to understand each other. I really want to be a painter or designer (I think I have a gift for it), but I probably have little time to develop those skills.
